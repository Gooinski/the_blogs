Daily Habits

Improvement through repatition.

Author: Chris Lipinski, Published: 20221203

Daily habits lead to improvement by repatition. I believe making time and finding reinforcing conditions and enviroments helps in forming better habit.

Start by doing small actions that gravitate to a habit you are trying to achieve goes along way to success rather than over thinking and delaying action.

Once you have completed something you feel has contributed to your desired goal step back. If you have more to do towards a postive habit it is a good idea to leave a desire to do more for the next day. It will help orientate you towards progressive success
