Creativity in Data Curation

Art of doing less

Author: Chris Lipinski, Published: 20221206

In my last article I touched on the constraints of data curation

The initial approach that is taken with data curation sets in place a momentum on the process with addressing issues that arise with data accurarcy and quality.

Setting correct conventions on what is important to get right and what can be overlooked in data curation and management is a kind of art.

Knowing in what context the final data sets are being used in should be the major guiding factor in setting these conventions.
